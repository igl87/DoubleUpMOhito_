# Buyer Personas for DoubleUp Fresh Mojito Green in Qatar

## Persona 1: Active Young Professional (Primary Target)
- **Name/Age/Gender**: Ahmed/Alia, 25-35, Male/Female.
- **Demographics**: Urban Qatari or expatriate (e.g., from India, Philippines, Europe); high income ($4,000+/month); lives in Doha.
- **Background**: Busy professional in finance/tech; fitness enthusiast (gym 3-4x/week); values health and convenience.
- **Behaviors**: Prefers low-sugar beverages; consumes energy drinks post-workout or during long days. Shops online/via apps; influenced by social media trends.
- **Pain Points**: Fatigue from heat/work; avoiding sugary crashes; seeking halal, natural options in Muslim-majority context.
- **Goals**: Stay energized and refreshed; align with wellness trends.
- **How DoubleUp Helps**: Fresh Mojito Green's natural mint-lime boost provides hydration and energy without guilt.
- **Marketing Touchpoints**: Instagram ads, gym partnerships, office vending.

## Persona 2: Expatriate Student/Socialite
- **Name/Age/Gender**: Maria/Jamal, 18-24, Female/Male.
- **Demographics**: International student or young expat; mid-income; multicultural background.
- **Background**: Studies/parties in Doha; active social life; follows global trends.
- **Behaviors**: Seeks fun, flavorful drinks; shares on TikTok; prefers alcohol-free alternatives for social events.
- **Pain Points**: Boring beverage options; need for quick pick-me-ups in hot climate.
- **Goals**: Enhance social experiences; maintain energy for studies/nights out.
- **How DoubleUp Helps**: Mojito flavor adds excitement; Qatari pride appeals to locals/expats.
- **Marketing Touchpoints**: Campus events, social media influencers, pop-up stalls.

## Persona 3: Health-Focused Family Head
- **Name/Age/Gender**: Fatima/Khalid, 35-45, Female/Male.
- **Demographics**: Qatari family; high disposable income; suburban Doha.
- **Background**: Parent balancing work/family; prioritizes health for kids.
- **Behaviors**: Buys in bulk from hypermarkets; seeks low-calorie family drinks.
- **Pain Points**: High-sugar risks; finding appealing, natural options.
- **Goals**: Promote family wellness; enjoy refreshing treats.
- **How DoubleUp Helps**: Low-sugar, green mojito as a family-friendly refreshment.
- **Marketing Touchpoints**: Supermarket promotions, family-oriented ads on TV/YouTube.

These personas are based on Qatar's demographics: Young, urban, health-conscious consumers driving beverage trends toward natural/low-sugar options.
