# Campaign Plan for Launching DoubleUp Fresh Mojito Green in Qatar

## Campaign Overview
- **Name**: "Double Up the Freshness" – Launching Fresh Mojito Green.
- **Objective**: Drive awareness and trials; achieve 50,000 units sold in first quarter.
- **Timeline**: Q2 2026 (April-June) – Align with summer heat.
- **Budget**: $500,000 (allocated: 40% digital, 30% events, 20% retail, 10% influencers).
- **Target Audience**: Young professionals, students, fitness enthusiasts (18-35).

## Strategies
- **Digital Marketing**: Run targeted ads on Instagram/TikTok (geo: Doha); content: Short videos of people "doubling up" energy in daily life.
- **Influencer Partnerships**: Collaborate with 10 Qatari influencers (fitness/lifestyle, 50K+ followers); sponsored posts/reviews.
- **Events & Activations**: Pop-up booths at malls (Villaggio), sports events; free samples at gyms.
- **Retail Promotions**: Shelf space in major stores; BOGO deals; in-store demos.
- **PR**: Press releases on local media (Qatar Tribune); highlight Qatari innovation.

## Tactics & Timeline
- **Week 1-2 (Pre-Launch)**: Teaser social posts; build hype.
- **Week 3-6 (Launch)**: Full ads rollout; events in Doha.
- **Week 7-12 (Sustain)**: User contests; retargeting ads.

## Measurement
- KPIs: Reach (1M impressions), Engagement (10% rate), Sales uplift, ROI.
- Tools: Google Analytics, social insights.

This plan leverages Qatar's trends: Health focus, digital savvy, and event-driven culture.
