# Customer Journey Map for DoubleUp Fresh Mojito Green in Qatar

## Stages
1. **Awareness**:
   - Touchpoints: Social media ads (Instagram/TikTok showing refreshing sips in Doha's heat), influencer partnerships (Qatari fitness bloggers), billboards near malls.
   - Customer Thoughts: "What's this new Qatari drink? Looks refreshing for summer."
   - Goal: Introduce as local, natural energy booster.

2. **Consideration**:
   - Touchpoints: Website/product page (doubleup.qa), in-store samples at hypermarkets (Lulu, Carrefour), email newsletters with health benefits.
   - Customer Thoughts: "Is it low-sugar? How does it taste compared to Red Bull?"
   - Goal: Educate on USPs (mojito flavor, natural ingredients, Qatari-made).

3. **Purchase**:
   - Touchpoints: Retail shelves, online delivery (Talabat, Snoonu), promo bundles (buy 2 get 1 free).
   - Customer Thoughts: "Easy to grab during grocery run; affordable premium."
   - Goal: Seamless buying; leverage convenience in high-income market.

4. **Retention**:
   - Touchpoints: Loyalty app (rewards for repeats), follow-up emails with recipes (e.g., mocktails), user-generated content contests.
   - Customer Thoughts: "This keeps me energized; love the flavor variety."
   - Goal: Build habit; encourage subscriptions.

5. **Advocacy**:
   - Touchpoints: Social shares (#DoubleUpYourDay), reviews on Google/Apps, referral programs.
   - Customer Thoughts: "Recommending to friends for workouts."
   - Goal: Turn customers into promoters via word-of-mouth in expat communities.

## Key Insights
- Qatar's hot climate amplifies need for refreshment at every stage.
- Digital-first for youth; in-person for families.
- Metrics: Track engagement (likes/shares), conversion (sales), NPS for loyalty.
